## Third-party code and attributions

This software uses open-source packages from the npm ecosystem. Compliance does not rely on `node_modules` in production; we ship the following in the repository:

- **[THIRD_PARTY_LICENSES.md](THIRD_PARTY_LICENSES.md)** – Index of third-party dependencies and their licenses (autogenerated).
- **[NOTICE.md](NOTICE.md)** – Aggregated notices and attributions, including upstream NOTICE content and CC-BY attributions.
- **`licenses/`** – Full license texts for the license families used by our dependencies.

Each dependency remains under its own license. Nothing in this project’s LICENSE alters the terms of those third-party licenses.

To regenerate the third-party index after adding or removing dependencies, run:

```bash
npm run licenses
```

(or `node scripts/generate-licenses.js`). The index is autogenerated; full license texts are shipped under `licenses/` and notices in NOTICE.md.
